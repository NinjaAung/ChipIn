The concept is very simple.

As simple as searching for a cause that you would like to support and chipping In.

Donations don’t help the people who need the most aid. There are thousands of non-profits that you can choose to give to, and the process of selecting which organization to support can feel like a daunting task. Fortunately, ChipIn conducts extensive research in order to determine which charities will most effectively use your money to help the world’s neediest people.

Through this extensive research we have developed an algorithm that sorts through a list of multiple charities so that when chippers chipin, their hard-earned money goes to the world’s most effective charities — effective in getting funds to those who most need support, and effective in using the funds most meaningfully to establish sustainable alleviation of poverty.
